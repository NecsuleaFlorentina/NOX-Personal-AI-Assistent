# NOX-Personal-AI-Assistent
NOX is a modern personal AI assistant built with Python, featuring a sleek dark-mode desktop interface. It combines conversational AI, voice recognition, reminders, notes, file search, weather, currency exchange, and music — all in one place.
What NOX Can Do
At its core, NOX is a conversational AI assistant. You can type anything into the chat and receive intelligent, context-aware responses powered by a large language model. But what makes NOX stand out is how many things it handles beyond simple chat. Every feature is accessible directly from the conversation window — just type or speak naturally, and NOX figures out what you need.

Voice Input
NOX supports fully hands-free interaction through its built-in voice recognition system. Powered by Deepgram's Nova-2 model — one of the most accurate speech-to-text engines available — NOX listens to your microphone in real time and transcribes what you say instantly. It supports the Romanian language natively, so you can speak naturally without worrying about mispronunciations or accents. Partial results appear as you speak, so the experience feels fluid and responsive, not laggy. Just press the microphone button and start talking — NOX will handle the rest.
Reminders
Never forget anything again. You can ask NOX to remind you about anything at any time, using natural language. Say something like "remind me to call mom at 6 PM" or "set a reminder for my meeting tomorrow at 10" and NOX will parse your request, save the reminder, and notify you at the right moment. All your reminders are visible in the dedicated Reminders section of the sidebar, where you can review, edit, or delete them at any time.

Notes
NOX includes a lightweight but powerful note-taking system. You can ask it to save a note mid-conversation — "save note: buy groceries and call the doctor" — and it will store it instantly. All your notes are accessible from the Notes section in the navigation panel. It's perfect for capturing quick thoughts, to-do items, or anything you want to remember later without switching to another app.

File Search
If you've ever spent too much time hunting for a file buried somewhere on your computer, NOX can help. The File Search module lets you search your local machine by name or keyword directly from the chat. Just ask NOX to find a file and it will scan your system and return the results in seconds.

Weather & Currency
NOX has live weather integration, so you can ask things like "what's the weather in Bucharest today?" or "will it rain tomorrow?" and get accurate, up-to-date forecasts. On top of that, NOX includes a currency exchange module — ask for any conversion between world currencies and get the latest rates instantly. Both features are powered by live data, so the information is always current.
